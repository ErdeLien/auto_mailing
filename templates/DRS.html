Below is the instruction of all related DRS documents that you need to provide to finish a DRS transfer.
 
If you would like to proceed with DRS transfers, there are some documents that you need to fill out:
  • DRS Transfer Form (Attached)
  • Due Diligence Checklist (Attached)
  • Sale Order Checklist (Attached). Please only answer questions Q1-Q16 in the first section.
For any of the 3 documents above, DO NOT sign any of them, and DO NOT convert Word Docs to PDFs.
 
Also there are documents that you need to provide:
  • Rule 144 Opinion Letter (ask your lawyer as this is a legal opinion) this is required if your stock is registered, but if your DRS transferred stock is not registered, you can ignore this.
  • Purchase agreement with the issuer/listed company
  • Prospectus of the issuer
  • Proof of payment of purchase, if applicable
  • Book entry account statement (an example is attached)
 
Please note that Curvature and AC Sunshine MAY require more documentations once DRS transfer submitted. In case of this we will notify you. Before all required documents are submitted to us, your DRS transfer will NOT BE processed. Please understand that this amount of work is necessary as DRS transfer requires diligent examination.
