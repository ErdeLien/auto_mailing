Case 1: if you are using domestic bank accounts and want to make ACH transfers, you need to:
  1. Connect your bank account to your Curvature account:
    1. In your dashboard, click Banking -> Bank Account -> Add Bank Account (on the top righ
    2. Select ACH as your Allowed Request Method and Plaid as your Approved Method
    3. Verify your mobile number and select either instant or manual as how you will link your bank accoun
  2. After you successfully linked your bank account, go to ACH/Wire Request (on the left sidebar of your page) -> Add ACH (on the upright corner) to make deposit
Please be notified that ACH transfers require at least 3 business days to be processed, and it has a limitation of $50,000 on daily transfer. Therefore, if your transaction is urgent or in large amounts, please use wire transfer (Case 2) instead!
 

Case 2: if you are an international customer and want to make a deposit, you need to use wire transfer. Please refer to the attached wire instruction for more information.

  • One thing you need to carefully notice is that, to successfully complete the wire transfer, you must include "For Further Credit" (FFC) account number in your transaction, when you submit it to the bank. It is mentioned and explained at the bottom of the wire instruction. This is very important to include FFC when you are writing for a transfer! If you do not include FFC in your transfer, Velox will not know which account this transfer is for, and your transfer will fail.

Please note that, even for case 2 (Wire transfer), it will take at least 1 business days for the money to be deposited. Your funds cannot arrive immediately, and there will be NO notification once the funds arrive. 

 
Especially:
  1. For ACH transfers, the maximum amount per day per customer is $50,000
  2. For wire transfers, there is no maximum amount cap, but you will be charged a fixed wire fee.
